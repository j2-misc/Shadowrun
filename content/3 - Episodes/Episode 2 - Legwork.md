# Summary
After a week, the group was contacted for another mission. After the [[The Blood Brothers|Blood Brothers]]' attack on the [[The Ancients|Ancients]]-owned [[Out-N-In]], the Ancients wanted revenge. According to [[Samantha]], there would be a transport of [[BTLs]] in **three days**. The party was to intercept the convoy (ice cream truck + pursuit vehicle), and either destroy or (preferably) steal them, making it known that the Ancients were behind the attack. Pursuant to this end, [[Chris Santiago]] was hired to help them. If they were successful, they'd all be walking away with 5,000 nuyen each.

After some detours, the group managed to put in some legwork in order to narrow down where the convoy was likely to be: a safehouse in [[Auburn]] where they were tuning BTLs.
