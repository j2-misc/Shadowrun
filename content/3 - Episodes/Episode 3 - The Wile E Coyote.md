# Summary
The party set up a trap for the [[The Blood Brothers|Blood Brothers]]' [[BTLs|BTL]] convoy. This proved incredibly effective, killing six people in roughly half as many seconds. Though the BTLs were destroyed, their main objective was complete and they were rewarded with **5,000 nuyen** and **10 karma** each.

Afterwards, [[Chris Santiago|Chris]] took the undamaged motorcycle and asked [[Detox]] to take out all the computers and fix it up for him. So she got into contact with one of [[Jane Doe|Jane]]'s discord kittens, [[Juan Santiago]] who offered his services for free due to the connections. [[Ghost Viper]] also brought [[Jane Doe|Jane]] to a club in order to buy a spell formula.
